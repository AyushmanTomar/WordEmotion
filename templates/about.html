About Me

Welcome to my website! I am passionate about leveraging technology to understand and interpret human emotions. With a background in machine learning and artificial intelligence, I have developed a deep neural network model that can predict emotions from text input.

My Project

This project utilizes a Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) network, a type of recurrent neural network (RNN) that is particularly effective for sequence prediction problems. By training this model on a diverse dataset, it can accurately identify and predict various human emotions based on the text provided.

How It Works

  • Deep Neural Network: The core of the project is a deep neural network that processes and analyzes text data.
  • LSTM: The LSTM architecture helps in capturing the context and nuances of the text, making the emotion prediction more accurate.
